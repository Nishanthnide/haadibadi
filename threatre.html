We believe theatre is an excellent medium and process in education. We do various workshops and programs for students, teachers and non-teaching staff. People react in a unique way to every new form of expression.



Theatre for children

We believe that Theatre creates an environment where a child’s eccentricities, fueled by their expansive imagination, are allowed to flourish, this builds confidence in a short period of time. They look to people they admire to tell them that it’s okay to be different in a world of normal
For students we do introductory theatre workshop (3days workshop), yearlong theatre program, summer camps, theatre to address behavioral issues etc which are specific to the need, age and the number of students.


Theatre for teachers

Theatre workshops have proved to be very helpful in breaking certain barriers among teachers. We conduct workshops for teachers for exploring the option of using theatre in their teaching, for developing theatre skills as teachers themselves use the faculties of performance – aural and visual in their teaching.


Theatre for Non Teaching Staff

Interpersonal skills are one of the important components in the school cultures that need to addressed for a healthy work culture. We have worked with top schools in Bangalore and the BBMP schools with excellent results.



We offer theatre based workshops in corporate training and make them experiential learning. From Induction programme to the leadership training programme theatre can be a great tool for the participants to experience the change and live it.



Induction Ignition

We provide the new employees a space to open up themselves. The workshop aims in introducing the employees in to the work culture and relationship values of the organization.


Empowering you

Theatre provides the right approach in identifying the conflicts, to overcome them in a subtle and practical way. It enables the employees in realising the importance of being the true team player that is productive for the individual growth and the team’s goal.


Therapeutic Theatre

Working professionals in corporate companies today are required to give their 100% to contribute to the growth of the organization and their own individual rise in career. Maintaining these high levels of work pressure can at times take toll on the physical and the personal levels. Theatre through interactive theatre and role playing can provide the best solution.


Theatre Training for Actors

You have wanted to be part of the stage and performance always but never found an opportunity or stage to do so? Here is your ticket to the show. Not to watch but prepare yourself to bring out the performer in you and set the stage on fire. This is aimed in providing basic acting training for corporate people that eventually culminates in the performance.



Community Theatre

Theatre, even in its most primitive forms has engaged in community, in reflecting upon the society it is part of. We offer theatre based training, street plays for issues of a number of rights based organizations who work at grassroot level such as garment and other labour union organizations in cities and for the tribal rights and youth development.
